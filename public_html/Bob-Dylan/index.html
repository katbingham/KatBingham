Bob Dylan

A Timeline of My Top Favorite Bob Dylan Albums:

  • 1964 - The Times They Are A-Changin'

    • Check out "The Times They Are A-Changin'" and "Boots of Spanish Leather"

  • 1964 - Another Side Of Bob Dylan

    • Check out "All I Really Want to Do", "Chimes of Freedom", and "It Ain't Me Babe"

  • 1965 - The Cutting Edge 1965-1966: The Bootleg Series, Vol.12(Deluxe Edition)

    • Check out the MANY versions of "She Belongs to Me"

  • 1965 - Bringing It All Back Home

    • Check out "Subterranean Homesick Blues", the original "She Belongs to Me", as well as the original "Mr. Tambourine Man", "Maggie's Farm" and "It's All Over Now, Baby Blue" should also make your list.

  • 1966 - Blonde on Blonde

    • Another fire album. Definitely listen to "Visions of Johanna" and "I Want You".

  • 1966 - No Direction Home: Bootleg Volume 7 (Movie Soundtrack)

    • The versions of "Mr. Tambourine Man" and "She Belongs to Me" here are my absolute favorite.

  • 1975 - Blood on the Tracks

    • The breakup album. Check out "Tangled up in Blue", "Simple Twist of Fate", and "You're Gonna Make Me Lonesome When You Go".

  • 1976 - Desire

    • "Hurricane" is a must listen.

  • 1991 - The Bootleg Series Volumes 1-3(Rare and Unreleased)1961-1991

    • This album is worth it JUST for "I'll Keep It with Mine"

  • 1997 - Time Out Of Mind

    • "Not Dark Yet" is a later works must listen.

  • 2001 - Love and Theft

    • Give "Mississippi" a listen.
